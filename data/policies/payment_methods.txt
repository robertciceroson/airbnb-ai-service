Payment methods accepted
We accept several payment method options. In most countries, you can pay with major credit cards, as well as debit cards that can be processed as credit cards. We also accept digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay, plus online services such as PayPal and Klarna. No matter how you choose to pay, stay safe by keeping payments on Airbnb.
Since accepted options vary by country or region, check the list of countries in this article to see which payment methods are available when you’re booking.
Pay over time with Klarna
Residents of the USA and Canada have the option to pay with Klarna, allowing you to pay over time, rather than all at once. Klarna accepts all major debit and credit cards–like Visa, Discover, Maestro, and Mastercard. Prepaid cards are not accepted. Learn more about
